Knowing how to code unlocks the world. Everywhere you look, the brightest innovators are using software to build their ideas, inspire action, and solve the world's toughest problems.

But coding know-how alone is not enough. The leaders of today and tomorrow have more than "hard skills"—they also know how to collaborate in groups, harness their creativity, think globally and across disciplines, and show grit in the face of failure. Enter Igneo.

Igneo is a learning experience worthy of the 21st century. And it's free.

RIGOR, COLLABORATION, SELF-DIRECTION

We believe that the best learning happens in communities defined by rigorous expectations, a culture of collaboration, and inspiring peers. You'll find that our classes don't quite feel like ordinary school, because school shouldn't be about jumping through hoops until graduation. Instead, it should nurture, challenge, and inspire.

FULL STACK KNOWLEDGE

From little or no experience, we'll take you through the fundamentals of computer science and writing efficient, working programs. And there's more: every step of the way, we'll tie your learning to student-directed projects. Your learning will have purpose, and you'll get the expertise to get hired—or better yet, start your own company.

TACKLE REAL PROBLEMS

The world doesn't need more excellent test takers, it needs more problem-solvers who are passionate about creating value, and have the expertise to make it happen. Igneo students have a calling to a higher cause, and we'll use design thinking to research, define, and solve tough problems and make impact in the community.

CREATIVITY, INVIGORATED

Creativity isn't a gift that's bestowed upon some: research shows that it can be honed over time. Doing so requires disciplined iteration, abandoning your fear of failure, embracing play, and working effectively with supportive teammates. Our design process and culture will unleash your creative potential.

COMMIT EFFORT, NOT MONEY

Learning so intensely is hard work, but extremely rewarding. We don't want finances to exclude anyone from the experience, so we're offering our course completely for free, laptop included. The catch? You have to bring your A-game every day, and commit your full time and effort to both the course and the internship.

Why code?

GET HIRED

Knowing how to code is one of the most in-demand skills in the global economy: with it, you can get hired at local businesses or companies large and small, all around the world.

SOLVE PROBLEMS

Increasingly, the solutions to the world's biggest problems involve technology as at least a part of the solution. Coding will become an invaluable part of your toolkit, open doors, and break down barriers.

EMPOWER YOURSELF

The most exciting part: the future will be heavily integrated with technology. Who knows what innovations wait just around the corner?
