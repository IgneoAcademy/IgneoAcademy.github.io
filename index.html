REIMAGINE SCHOOL

We believe that the best learning happens in communities defined by rigorous expectations, a culture of collaboration, and talented peers who inspire you to be your best. Igneo creates such communities of world-class teachers and students, provides high quality instruction, and enables students to solve the problems that they're passionate about. School shouldn't be about jumping through hoops until graduation; it should nurture, challenge, and inspire.

LEARN THE FULL STACK

From little or no prior experience, we'll take you through the fundamentals of computer science, algorithmic problem-solving, and of course, writing efficient, working code. But we also know that software development isn't just about writing good code, it's about applying computing principles to the real world. Every step of the way, we'll tie our learning to student-directed projects, so your learning has purpose.

REFINE YOUR CREATIVITY

Creativity isn't a gift that's bestowed upon some and not others: research shows that like any skill, it can be honed over time.
