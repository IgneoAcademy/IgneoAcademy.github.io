THE IGNEO TEAM

The Igneo team represents diverse interests and backgrounds, together boasting years of software engineering, teaching, and leadership experience.

RAFI KHAN, Founder / Head Teacher

Rafi's been teaching programming for years through the student organization HackYale, which he directed his junior year. He's coded for tech companies ranging from Google to his own startups, graduated a Yale Education Studies Scholar, and served on Yale's Committee for Online Education for two years, helping decide university-wide online education policy with a top professors and administrators. He's a lead teacher at iXperience, a tech bootcamp in Cape Town geared toward foreign students, and the CTO and Projects Director of GAKKO, an global education company based in Japan.

CEZAR MOCAN, Teaching / Curriculum Development

Cezar started studying computer science more than ten years ago, has since engineered at Google Summer of Code, Twitter and Palantir Technologies, and won two Silver medals for the Romania at the Balkan Olympiad in Informatics. As far as teaching goes, he trained high schoolers for the National Olympiad in Informatics for created his own workshops on product design.

ABDEL MORSY, Design / Marketing

Growing up as a textile salesman for his grandfather’s company in Egypt, Abdel sold textiles and attended conferences and negotiations with Egyptian business leaders. Since leaving Egypt, he’s worked as a fine art salesman and negotiator in Istanbul, launched a spice-subscription start-up (The 3rd Shaker), and designed logos, posters, banners, brochures, and abstract art projects for dozens of causes, most prominently for anti-regime film collectives during the Egyptian revolution. Abdel studies Graphic Design and Cognitive Science at Yale.
